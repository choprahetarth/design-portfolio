Research and Development of Smart Fruit Quality Manager

Undergraduate Research Project under Dr. PS Rana , Dr. AK Singh and Dr. RK Singla of TIET Patiala

Developed a mechatronic system for segregating fruits based on their grade by using Spectroscopy and Machine Learning. The fruits are fed to the system and the machine classifies the fruits using Azure Custom Vision API and then using Spectroscopy and a custom gradient boosted ensemble, it grades the fruits into separate classes. The entire system works with the help of an Arduino Mega controller and a Raspberry Pi.

Concrete Compressive-Strength Prediction Algorithm using Machine Learning

Research Project under Dr. PS Rana of TIET Patiala and Dr. Nishtha Hooda of IIIT Una

Measuring the concrete's compressive strength is an ever-important task in the civil engineering industry. A collaborative effort has been put up between three institutes to predict the compressive strength of concrete samples using advanced gradient boosting methods, after extracting features such as Water %, Superplasticizer %, etc. A MAPE of 1.2% was overall found after the training of the model.
